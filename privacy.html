3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are based in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK or another jurisdiction with equivalent data protection law, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance — to provide the Platform services you have subscribed to
  • Legitimate interests — to operate and improve the Platform, prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Legal obligation — to comply with applicable laws including tax and financial recordkeeping requirements
  • Consent — for marketing communications, where you have opted in

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account and deliver Platform services
  • To send you alerts, notifications and service updates you have configured
  • To process your subscription payments and issue invoices
  • To respond to support requests and communications
  • To analyse usage patterns and improve the Platform experience
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse or security threats
  • To send marketing communications where you have consented (you may withdraw at any time)

5. Data We Display — Public Football Data

Transfer365 displays aggregated public information about professional footballers, clubs and the transfer market. This data is sourced from publicly available records and licensed third-party databases. Such data relates to individuals in their professional capacity as public figures in the football industry and is processed on the basis of legitimate interests for informational and analytical purposes.

Important: All such data is provided for advisory purposes only and may contain errors, omissions or inaccuracies. Transfer365 does not warrant the accuracy of any data relating to players, contracts, injuries or financial positions of clubs. Users must independently verify all data before acting on it.

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only as follows:

  • LemonSqueezy — payment processing. Their privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • Supabase — database and authentication infrastructure. Data is stored on their servers under a data processing agreement.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery.
  • Analytics providers — aggregated, anonymised usage data only.
  • Law enforcement or regulatory bodies — where legally required.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account remains active and for up to 12 months following account deletion or inactivity, unless a longer period is required by law. Payment records are retained for the period required by applicable tax law (typically 7 years). Usage logs are retained for 90 days.

You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time by contacting support@transfer365.net. We will process deletion requests within 30 days, subject to any legal retention requirements.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction, including the United States, where our infrastructure providers operate. Where data is transferred outside the EEA or UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) to protect your data in accordance with applicable law.

9. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — request that we limit processing in certain circumstances
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@transfer365.net. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

10. Cookies and Tracking

Transfer365 uses the following types of cookies and local storage:

We do not use advertising cookies or sell data to advertising networks.

No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you in accordance with applicable law.

Transfer365 is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove it.
