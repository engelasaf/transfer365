Questions

Everything you need to know.

Do I need to be a FIFA-licensed agent?

Transfer365 is designed for FIFA-licensed football agents and registered agencies. All FFAR commission calculations and compliance tools are built to current FIFA regulations.

Where does the player and club data come from?

Transfer365 aggregates from multiple sources including Transfermarkt valuations, public contract databases, and club financial disclosures. Valuations always show the source range so you know the confidence level.

How quickly do real-time triggers fire?

Injury and transfer request triggers typically fire within 2–6 hours of a confirmed public event. Contract expiry alerts run daily. Manager sackings aim for under 1 hour from confirmed news.

Can I cancel or change plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade instantly from your account page. Annual plans include a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you cancel, you keep access until the end of your billing period.

Who handles payments and invoicing?

Payments are processed by LemonSqueezy (our Merchant of Record). They handle EU VAT and tax compliance automatically. You receive a proper invoice for every payment.
